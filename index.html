About Me

Hello! My name is Renat Norderhaug and I am a Software Engineer currently living in San Jose, CA. I am working as a Software Engineer at Unified an iHeartMedia company. I made this website to showcase some of my work, and show who I am.

My Story

My passion for coding started at an extremely young age. I was 10 years old when I first wrote code for a standalone Java application. At the time, I really had no idea about the core concepts behind computer science, but I was extremely interested in video games. I would spend days, weeks, and months changing the look of my game's UI. My friends and I would play the game together, and I was and still am obsessed with my computer and building things. I would host a private server on my laptop and use Google to find tutorials on game development so that my friends and I could play video games together. In college, I was extremely interested in low-level programming with the Arduino. The first time I finally understood how the circuitry worked, I was constantly obsessing over reading the Arduino's manual and understanding every single bit on every single register. When I got my first phototransistor to light up, it made my face light up as well.

The Rest

When I am not working, my hobbies include flying drones, hiking, working out, reading, basketball, and learning how to manage my finances. I am a very enthusiastic, self-motivated individual, who is continuously improving.

Projects

Some projects I have worked on throughout my career.

Nevada RAVE

Mobile first web app for Nevada RAVE, used in-house by the company to check in/check out staff members and store COVID-related medical information. You can find a demo here.

Airbnb calendar microservice

System design for a calendar microservice utilizing PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Nginx, AWS EC2, S3, Docker, New Relic, Mocha, and Chai. I successfully implemented horizontal web scaling to handle over 100 million records of data. You can find a link to the repository here.

Trip planning microservice

Trip planning microservice utilizing React, styled-components, Node.js, Express.js, Webpack, MySQL, AWS EC2, Jest, and Enzyme. I integrated the Google Maps API to display a modal showing the location of a vacation. You can find a link to the repository here.
